# Logic ERP don hang, khach hang, ke toan va cong viec

## Nguyen tac chinh

Moi don hang bat buoc phai gan voi mot khach hang.

Moi phat sinh tien, cong viec, di ra ngoai, cham cong lien quan den don hang neu co thi phai gan truc tiep voi don hang do.

Moi khoan thu/chi/phat sinh bat buoc phai co chung tu.

Ke toan khong nen phai di tim don hang bang tay. Moi thong bao thu/chi/dat coc lien quan den don hang phai co link mo thang ve don hang de doi chieu va xac nhan.

## Khach hang va don hang

Don hang khong duoc tao neu khong co khach hang.

Ke ca khach le cung bat buoc phai tao ho so khach hang truoc khi tao don. Khong cho tao don hang khong co khach hang.

Khach hang can quan ly theo nhieu loai, khong chi la mot ten va mot so dien thoai.

Loai khach hang co the gom:

- Khach le / ca nhan.
- Dai ly.
- Nha thau.
- Chu dau tu.
- Cong ty / to chuc.
- Khach hang noi bo neu can.

Thong tin khach hang toi thieu voi khach ca nhan:

- Ma khach hang.
- Ten khach hang.
- So dien thoai.
- Dia chi.
- Ghi chu.
- Lich su don hang.
- Cong no neu co.

Thong tin khach hang to chuc / chu dau tu / cong ty nen co them:

- Ten phap ly.
- Ten giao dich.
- Ma so thue neu co.
- Dia chi tru so.
- Dia chi cong trinh/du an.
- Nguoi dai dien.
- Nhom khach hang.
- Nguon khach.
- Han muc cong no neu co.
- Dieu khoan thanh toan mac dinh.
- Nhan vien phu trach chinh.
- Trang thai hop tac.

Khach hang co the co nhieu nguoi lien he.

Moi nguoi lien he can luu:

- Ho ten.
- Chuc vu.
- Phong ban.
- So dien thoai.
- Email/Zalo neu co.
- Vai tro lien he: mua hang, ky thuat, ke toan, quan ly, cong truong, phap ly.
- La lien he chinh hay lien he phu.
- Ghi chu.

Voi khach hang dang chu dau tu/cong ty, viec co nhieu phong ban va nhieu so dien thoai la bat buoc de van hanh thuc te.

Vi du:

- Phong mua hang: tiep nhan bao gia va don hang.
- Phong ke toan: xac nhan thanh toan, cong no, hoa don.
- Phong ky thuat: xac nhan ban ve, thong so.
- Ban quan ly cong truong: dieu phoi giao/lap.
- Lanh dao/nguoi duyet: duyet gia va dieu kien thanh toan.

Khi tao don hang, can chon:

- Khach hang.
- Dia chi giao/lap/cong trinh.
- Nguoi lien he chinh cua don.
- Nguoi lien he ke toan neu co.
- Nguoi lien he ky thuat/cong truong neu co.

Moi don hang can luu:

- Ma don hang.
- Khach hang.
- Nguoi lien he chinh.
- Dia chi thuc hien/giao hang.
- Nguoi phu trach ban hang.
- Ky thuat phu trach neu co.
- Trang thai don.
- Gia tri don hang ban dau.
- Gia tri phat sinh tang/giam.
- Tong gia tri phai thu.
- Tong da thu.
- Con phai thu.

## Phat sinh theo don hang

Moi phat sinh neu khach hang tra thi cong vao tien cua don hang do.

Vi du:

- Khach them phu kien.
- Khach doi mau mat tien chenhlech.
- Khach yeu cau lap them hang muc.
- Khach chiu phi van chuyen/phat sinh.

Tat ca cac khoan nay phai lam tang gia tri phai thu cua don hang.

Cong thuc:

```text
Tong phai thu don hang = Gia tri don ban dau + Phat sinh khach tra - Giam tru
Con phai thu = Tong phai thu don hang - Tong da thu
```

## Phat sinh chi phi cong ty

Neu khoan phat sinh do cong ty tra thi khong cong vao tien don hang khach phai tra. Khoan do duoc ghi vao chi phi cong ty.

Vi du:

- Chi phi sua sai do noi bo.
- Chi phi di lai do cong ty chiu.
- Chi phi vat tu hao hut khong thu cua khach.
- Chi phi nhan su, tang ca, phu cap.
- Chi phi mua ngoai.

Khoan nay can gan duoc voi don hang neu co, de xem lai lai/lo theo don.

Cong thuc lai lo don hang:

```text
Loi nhuan don hang = Tong khach phai tra - Gia von vat tu - Chi phi cong ty gan voi don
```

## Chung tu bat buoc

Moi khoan thu, chi, dat coc, tam ung, hoan tien, chi phi phat sinh deu phai co chung tu.

Chung tu can co:

- Ma chung tu.
- Loai chung tu.
- Ngay phat sinh.
- So tien.
- Nguoi tao.
- Nguoi xac nhan.
- Trang thai.
- File dinh kem neu co.
- Link den don hang neu chung tu lien quan don hang.
- Link den khach hang/nhan vien/nha cung cap neu co.

Chung tu co the la:

- Hoa don.
- Phieu thu.
- Phieu chi.
- Bien lai chuyen khoan.
- Anh chup giao dich ngan hang.
- Hoa don/chung tu mua hang.
- Giay de nghi thanh toan.
- Giay tam ung/hoan ung.
- Tep dinh kem khac duoc ke toan chap nhan.

## Tien coc don hang

Neu co khoan dat coc cho don hang, NVKD tao chung tu dat coc gan truc tiep voi don hang.

Chung tu dat coc can co:

- Ma don hang.
- Khach hang.
- So tien coc.
- Ngay coc.
- Hinh thuc thanh toan.
- Anh/tep chung tu neu co.
- Ghi chu.
- Trang thai: cho ke toan xac nhan, da xac nhan, tu choi.

Sau khi tao, he thong gui thong bao cho ke toan.

Ke toan bam vao thong bao thi mo thang chung tu dat coc va don hang lien quan, khong phai tim don hang bang tay.

Ke toan xac nhan xong, so tien coc duoc cong vao tong da thu cua don hang.

Don hang chi duoc di tiep sang cac buoc xu ly chinh sau khi tien coc da duoc ke toan xac nhan, tru truong hop co ngoai le duoc quan ly/giam doc phe duyet.

Khach hang bat buoc phai dat coc thi don moi duoc chuyen sang ky thuat/kho xu ly.

Muc coc toi thieu do quan ly/admin cau hinh sau theo chinh sach cong ty. Co the la so tien co dinh hoac ty le phan tram tren gia tri don hang tuy cau hinh.

Neu coc chua duoc xac nhan:

- Don co the o trang thai cho xac nhan coc.
- Khong day sang ky thuat/kho/san xuat nhu mot don chinh thuc.
- NVKD can theo doi va bo sung chung tu neu ke toan tu choi.

## Link nguoc tu ke toan ve don hang

Moi khoan tien lien quan don hang phai mo duoc ve don hang.

Vi du:

- Ke toan nhan thong bao: "Don DH-000123 co coc 10.000.000".
- Bam vao thong bao.
- He thong mo chi tiet chung tu coc.
- Trong chi tiet co link den don DH-000123.
- Ke toan co the xem don, khach hang, tong tien, lich su thu/chi va xac nhan ngay.

## Cong viec va di ra ngoai

Cong viec neu lien quan don hang thi phai gan voi don hang.

Vi du:

- Di do kich thuoc.
- Di lap dat.
- Di bao hanh.
- Di giao hang.
- Di gap khach.
- Xu ly phat sinh tai cong trinh.

Ra ngoai gap khach duoc xem la cong viec, khong mac dinh la chi phi hay ngay cong dac biet neu khong co quy dinh rieng.

Cong viec di gap khach khong bat buoc quan ly duyet truoc, nhung nhan vien phai bao/tao cong viec de cong ty nam duoc lich lam viec va lich su cham soc khach hang.

Moi cong viec can luu:

- Ma cong viec.
- Loai cong viec.
- Don hang lien quan neu co.
- Khach hang lien quan neu co.
- Nhan vien phu trach.
- Thoi gian bat dau/ket thuc.
- Dia diem.
- Ket qua.
- Anh/tep dinh kem neu co.
- Chi phi phat sinh neu co.

Neu cong viec co chi phi:

- Neu khach tra: tao phat sinh thu cho don hang.
- Neu cong ty tra: tao chi phi cong ty, co the gan voi don hang de tinh lai/lo.

Chi phi cong ty do ke toan kiem soat va xac nhan.

Chi phi di ngoai do nhan vien khai bat buoc phai co chung tu/hinh anh/bien lai hop le thi ke toan moi xem xet xac nhan.

Doanh thu don hang va doanh so tong cong ty la thong tin nhay cam. Chi giam doc/quan ly duoc phan quyen moi duoc xem doanh so tong va bao cao doanh thu tong hop.

## Phan loai tien

Can tach ro:

| Loai tien | Tac dong |
|---|---|
| Khach tra theo don | Cong vao tong da thu hoac tong phai thu cua don |
| Khach phai tra them | Cong vao gia tri don hang |
| Cong ty chi cho don | Cong vao chi phi don hang |
| Cong ty chi chung | Cong vao chi phi cong ty, khong gan don |
| Hoan tien cho khach | Giam tong da thu hoac tao chung tu hoan tien |
| Tam ung nhan vien | Gan nhan vien, co the gan don neu di viec cho don |

## Cong no don hang va hoa hong

Khi lap don hang, can ghi ro dieu kien thanh toan va du kien thu tien.

Thong tin cong no don hang can co:

- Tong gia tri phai thu.
- Tien coc du kien.
- Ngay du kien coc.
- So tien con lai.
- Ngay du kien thu phan con lai.
- Dieu kien thanh toan: khi giao hang, sau lap dat, theo tien do, theo hop dong, ghi chu rieng.
- Trang thai cong no: chua thu, da thu mot phan, da thu du, qua han.

Khach tra theo don la phan quan ly cong no cua don hang.

Neu khach tra nhieu lan, moi lan phai co chung tu rieng va ke toan xac nhan.

Hoa hong cho nhan vien chi duoc tinh khi don hang da thu het tien theo quy dinh.

Quy tac:

```text
Chi tinh hoa hong khi don hang = da thu du / da chot cong no
```

Neu don hang con no, hoa hong cua nhan vien lien quan chua duoc tinh hoac chi duoc tinh theo chinh sach rieng neu cong ty cho phep.

Quyen giam gia/giam tru don hang do admin cau hinh theo tung chuc vu/phong ban/nguoi phu trach. Khong mac dinh tat ca nhan vien ban hang deu duoc giam gia.

## Phat sinh doanh thu cua don hang

Phat sinh khach tra chi duoc dua sang ke toan sau khi khach hang da chap nhan.

Luong de xuat:

```text
NVKD/Ky thuat tao de xuat phat sinh -> Khach xac nhan/chap nhan -> Chuyen ke toan theo doi thu -> Ke toan xac nhan khi khach thanh toan
```

Sale phai thao luan va thong nhat voi khach truoc. Sau khi khach chap nhan phat sinh, sale moi bao ke toan de dua vao theo doi thu.

Ke toan la nguoi duyet/xac nhan phat sinh khach tra ve mat chung tu va cong no.

Neu khach chua chap nhan:

- Khoan phat sinh chi nam o trang thai de xuat.
- Chua cong vao tong phai thu chinh thuc.
- Chua gui ke toan nhu mot khoan phai thu chinh thuc.

## Workflow de xuat

```text
Tao khach hang -> Tao don hang -> Tao bao gia/yeu cau -> Thu coc -> Ke toan xac nhan coc -> Ky thuat chot BOM -> Duyet don -> Giu kho/xuat kho/san xuat -> Cong viec di ngoai/lap dat -> Thu phan con lai -> Hoan tat
```

## Phan quyen de xuat

| Vai tro | Quyen chinh |
|---|---|
| NVKD | Tao khach hang, tao don hang, tao chung tu coc cho don, tao yeu cau phat sinh khach tra |
| Ky thuat | Chot thong so, chot BOM, tao/chinh cong viec ky thuat |
| Kho | Giu kho, xuat kho, cap nhat ton |
| Ke toan | Xac nhan tien coc, thu/chi, cong no, chi phi, bang luong, duyet nghi phep/di muon de tinh luong |
| Quan ly | Duyet don, duyet phat sinh lon, duyet hoan tien, xem bao cao |
| Admin | Cau hinh he thong, phan quyen |

## Cham cong, nghi phep va di muon

Du lieu cham cong goc nen lay tu may cham cong.

May cham cong la nguon du lieu tho, ghi nhan:

- Nhan vien.
- Ngay.
- Gio vao.
- Gio ra.
- Thiet bi cham cong.
- Trang thai dong bo.

He thong khong nen tinh luong truc tiep tu du lieu tho neu chua qua xu ly bang cong.

Luong thang nen tinh tu bang cong da chot.

Luong xu ly theo luong:

```text
May cham cong -> Du lieu cham cong tho -> Bang cong tam tinh -> Ke toan/nguoi duoc phan quyen duyet dieu chinh -> Bang cong chot -> Bang luong
```

### Nghi phep

Nhan vien co the tao don xin nghi phep.

Don nghi phep can co:

- Nhan vien.
- Ngay nghi.
- Loai nghi: co phep, khong luong, nghi om, nghi viec rieng.
- Ly do.
- File dinh kem neu co.
- Trang thai: cho duyet, da duyet, tu choi.
- Nguoi duyet.

Chi don nghi phep da duyet moi duoc tinh vao bang cong va bang luong.

Neu chua duyet, ngay do van duoc xem la vang/chua hop le khi tinh bang cong tam.

Don nghi phep can phan biet:

- Nghi co luong.
- Nghi khong luong.
- Nghi phep nam.
- Nghi dot xuat/bat kha khang.

Ke toan duyet nghi phep va quyet dinh hinh thuc tinh luong cua ngay nghi: co luong hoac khong luong theo quy dinh cong ty.

Nghi phep do ke toan duyet truc tiep la du dieu kien ghi nhan vao bang cong/bang luong, khong bat buoc quan ly truc tiep duyet truoc neu cong ty khong yeu cau.

Can co quy tac so lan/ngay nghi hop le trong thang.

Vi du:

- Moi thang duoc chap nhan toi da bao nhieu lan di muon/ve som co ly do.
- Moi thang duoc bao nhieu ngay nghi co luong neu con phep.
- Truong hop bat kha khang thi ke toan xem xet va duyet rieng.

Voi ngay nghi co ke hoach, nhan vien phai tao don xin truoc toi thieu tu ngay hom truoc, tru khi la truong hop dot xuat/bat kha khang.

Truong hop dot xuat/bat kha khang:

- Nhan vien tao don bo sung som nhat co the.
- Ke toan xem xet ly do va chung tu neu co.
- Chi khi duoc duyet moi duoc tinh luong cho ngay do theo loai nghi duoc phe duyet.

### Xin den muon / ve som

Nhan vien co the tao don xin den muon hoac ve som.

Don den muon/ve som can co:

- Nhan vien.
- Ngay ap dung.
- Gio du kien den muon hoac ve som.
- Ly do.
- Trang thai: cho duyet, da duyet, tu choi.
- Nguoi duyet.

Chi don da duyet moi duoc chap nhan khi tinh cong va tinh luong.

Neu khong duoc duyet, he thong tinh theo du lieu may cham cong va quy tac phat/di muon mac dinh.

So phut di muon/ve som bi tinh phat do admin cau hinh sau theo quy dinh cong ty.

Don xin den muon/ve som nen duoc tao truoc toi thieu tu ngay hom truoc doi voi truong hop co ke hoach.

Truong hop dot xuat/bat kha khang:

- Nhan vien tao don giai trinh.
- Ke toan xem xet va duyet neu hop le.
- Neu duoc duyet thi ngay cong/gio cong duoc tinh theo quy tac cong ty.
- Neu khong duoc duyet thi tinh la di muon/ve som theo may cham cong.

### Vai tro ke toan trong cham cong va luong

Ke toan hoac nguoi duoc phan quyen la nguoi xac nhan cac don nghi phep/den muon/ve som co duoc chap nhan de tinh luong hay khong.

Can tach ro:

| Du lieu | Nguon | Anh huong luong khi nao |
|---|---|---|
| Cham cong vao/ra | May cham cong | Sau khi dong bo vao bang cong |
| Nghi phep | Don xin nghi | Chi khi da duyet |
| Den muon/ve som | Don xin phep | Chi khi da duyet |
| Dieu chinh cong tay | Ke toan/quan ly | Chi khi co ly do va nguoi duyet |
| Bang cong thang | Tong hop tu cac nguon tren | Sau khi chot moi tao bang luong |

### Kiem soat cham cong

- Du lieu may cham cong khong nen sua truc tiep.
- Neu can sua, tao dong dieu chinh bang cong co ly do va nguoi duyet.
- Bang cong da chot thi khong duoc sua truc tiep.
- Neu can sua bang cong da chot, phai mo lai ky cong hoac tao phieu dieu chinh.
- Bang luong phai luu snapshot bang cong tai thoi diem tinh.
- Sau khi bang luong da duyet, sua cham cong khong duoc tu dong lam doi luong cu.

Ke toan duoc xem bang cong cua tat ca nhan vien.

Nhan vien chi duoc xem bang cong cua chinh minh.

Giam doc duoc xem bang cong tong the cua tat ca nhan vien.

Bang cong thang duoc he thong tu tong hop vao dau thang sau. Nhan vien co thoi han den 8h sang ngay dau tien cua thang sau de xem va doi soat bang cong cua minh.

Neu nhan vien thay sai trong thoi gian doi soat:

- Nhan vien phai lien he ke toan.
- Ke toan kiem tra du lieu may cham cong, don nghi phep, don den muon/ve som va cac dieu chinh lien quan.
- Neu hop le, ke toan dieu chinh/duyet truoc khi chot bang cong.

Sau thoi han doi soat, bang cong co the duoc chot de tinh luong.

Neu bang luong da duyet nhung phat hien sai do loi he thong, nhan vien/ke toan tao yeu cau gui admin de kiem tra va sua he thong theo quy trinh duoc phe duyet.

Phep nam va phep ton neu co se duoc xu ly trong phan quyet toan cuoi nam.

Tam ung nhan vien trong pham vi hien tai duoc hieu la tam ung luong.

## Kiem soat quan trong

- Khong co khach hang thi khong tao don.
- Khach le van phai co ho so khach hang.
- Khach hang to chuc/chu dau tu nen co it nhat mot nguoi lien he chinh.
- Don hang nen chon ro nguoi lien he va dia chi thuc hien.
- Khong co chung tu thi khong ghi nhan thu/chi.
- Chung tu lien quan don hang phai link ve don hang.
- Ke toan xac nhan thu/chi moi cap nhat so tien chinh thuc.
- NVKD co the tao thong tin coc, nhung ke toan la nguoi xac nhan.
- Coc phai duoc ke toan xac nhan thi don hang moi duoc di tiep sang xu ly chinh.
- Muc coc toi thieu do quan ly/admin cau hinh.
- Phat sinh khach tra lam tang tien don hang.
- Phat sinh khach tra phai duoc khach chap nhan truoc khi dua sang ke toan theo doi thu.
- Ke toan duyet/xac nhan phat sinh khach tra sau khi sale da thong nhat voi khach.
- Phat sinh cong ty tra lam tang chi phi cong ty hoac chi phi don hang.
- Chi phi cong ty do ke toan kiem soat.
- Doanh thu tong cong ty chi giam doc/quan ly duoc phan quyen moi duoc xem.
- Quyen giam gia/giam tru do admin cau hinh theo chuc vu.
- Cham cong thang phai lay tu bang cong da chot, khong lay truc tiep tu du lieu tho cua may cham cong.
- Nghi phep, den muon, ve som chi duoc tinh luong khi da duoc duyet.
- Nhan vien chi xem bang cong cua minh; ke toan xem duoc tat ca bang cong.
- Giam doc xem duoc bang cong tong the.
- Hoa hong nhan vien chi duoc tinh khi don hang da thu het tien theo quy dinh.
- Tam ung nhan vien la tam ung luong.
- Moi thay doi tien phai co audit log.

## Cac diem con can chot them

- Khi huy don, tien coc se hoan toan bo, khau tru chi phi, hay xu ly theo tung truong hop?
- Bang cong da chot co cho mo lai khong, hay chi tao phieu dieu chinh?
- Bang luong da duyet neu sai do nghiep vu thi dieu chinh trong thang cu hay tao khoan bu/tru o thang sau?
